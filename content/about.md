Hi, I'm Mike, a DevOps engineer. Even though I've been interested in computer science since I was a kid tinkering with computers, my professional journey started in 2021.

I work primarily with Kubernetes. In my previous role, I played a key part in developing and deploying a Kubernetes Platform as a Service (PaaS) project, which gave me deep hands-on experience with container orchestration and infrastructure automation. These days, I focus on developing and maintaining a catalog of self-service offerings—things like PostgreSQL and Redis—that make it easier for our customers to manage their services on Kubernetes.

When I'm not knee-deep in YAML files, you'll find me on the baseball field playing first base and outfield for the Zurich Barracudas. Baseball provides the perfect balance to my technical work and keeps me active.
